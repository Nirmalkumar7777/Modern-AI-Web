Automated Financial Market Surveillance & Insider Trading Detection

Overview

Automated Financial Market Surveillance & Insider Trading Detection is an AI-powered web application designed to monitor financial markets, identify suspicious trading activities, detect potential insider trading patterns, and assist analysts with intelligent investigation workflows.

The platform combines Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Generative AI, and Agentic AI capabilities to provide real-time market surveillance, risk assessment, anomaly detection, and regulatory reporting.

---

Problem Statement

Financial markets generate massive volumes of trading data every second. Traditional monitoring systems often struggle to identify sophisticated insider trading schemes, market manipulation activities, and suspicious transactions in real time.

This project aims to build an intelligent surveillance system that:

- Monitors market transactions continuously
- Detects anomalous trading behavior
- Identifies potential insider trading activities
- Analyzes financial news and market sentiment
- Generates AI-powered investigation reports
- Supports compliance and regulatory requirements
